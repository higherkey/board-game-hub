How to Play: Wisecrack

1

Write Wisecracks

Everyone gets two funny prompts (e.g., "The worst thing to say at a funeral"). Type in the funniest, weirdest, or most ridiculous answer you can think of!

2

Head-to-Head Battle

Two answers for the same prompt go head-to-head on the big screen. Nobody knows who wrote what until after the voting!

3

Vote & Win

Everyone else votes for their favorite answer. Win the battle to earn points! Get 100% of the votes for a Wisecrack Bonus!
