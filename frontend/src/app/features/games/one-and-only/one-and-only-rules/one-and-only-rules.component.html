How to Play: One and Only

1
The Mystery Word

One player is the **Guesser**. Everyone else sees a mystery word that the Guesser must identify.

2
Give Your Clue

Clue Givers write or draw **ONE** unique clue on their phone plaque. Be creative but careful!

3
Elimination Phase

If two or more players give the **SAME** clue (e.g. both write "Water"), those clues are **ELIMINATED** and the Guesser never sees them!

4
The Big Guess

The Guesser looks at the remaining unique clues and makes a single guess. If they're right, the whole team wins the round!

"Work together to keep your clues unique!"
