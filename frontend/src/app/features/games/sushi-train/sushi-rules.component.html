🥢 How to Play Sushi Train!

Game Flow

The game lasts 3 rounds. In each round, you are dealt a hand of cards.

  • Pick: Choose 1 card from your hand.
  • Reveal: Everyone reveals their card simultaneously.
  • Pass: Pass your remaining cards to the left.

Repeat until all cards are drafted!

Scoring Guide

🍣

Nigiri (Egg/Salmon/Squid)

Pure points: Egg (1), Salmon (2), Squid (3).

🥣

Wasabi

The next Nigiri you play is worth triple points!

🍙

Maki Rolls

Most icons: 6 pts. Second most: 3 pts. (Ties split points).

🍤

Tempura

Set of 2: 5 pts. Single card: 0 pts.

🐟

Sashimi

Set of 3: 10 pts. Less than 3: 0 pts.

🥟

Dumplings

More is better! Scale: 1, 3, 6, 10, 15 pts.

🍮

Puddin' (Dessert)

Scored AFTER 3 rounds. Most: +6 pts. Least: -6 pts.

🥢

Chopsticks

Swap this from your table back into your hand to pick 2 cards at once.

Draft wisely. Happy dining! 🏮
